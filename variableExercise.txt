What is the difference between var and let?
	You can both reassign and redeclare with var, but with let you cannot redeclare.

What is the difference between var and const?
	You can both reassign and redeclare with var, but with const you cannot do either.

What is the difference between let and const?
	Let allows you to reassign but not redeclare, whereas const won't let you do either.

What is hoisting?
	Using var, variables are lifted to the top and declared right away, even before initialization.
